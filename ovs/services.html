During Floods

Evacuating the people to safer places.Providing basic facilities such as food ,water and shelther.We tries to put our full efforts till the last person is rescued.

During Fire disasters

Tries to settle down the fire as early as possible. Decreases the damage rate of both the lives and the property, helps immediately since volunteers are near to the affected region.

Rescuing the people underneath the building and providing basic first aid to the injured people and moving the people to safer places and providing temporary shelther facilities.
